What Do I Do?

UX-First Designs

What we know vs. what we think our users know vs. what they really know are never the same. This is why building your foundation off of a solid UX strategy is so critical to building effective User Interfaces in 2019. This is why I always employ the full UX design process with each new project. Starting with why guarantees a better UI. Each time.

Full Stack Coding

My core strategy when developing & deploying any software is to implement Agile principles towards delivering an exceptional user experience to anybody in the world, on any device. The way I do this is by constantly seeking knowledge on market / software trends to make better decisions in the overall design / functionality process.

Digital Strategy

With the training I received from Tai Lopez three years ago, I decided to start up my own digital marketing firm. As M3 Digital grows, the market changes. What does it take to win? I am constantly using my resources and seeking out the best ideas. and that knowledge along with those relationships play a huge role in my business today.

Apple Development

As somebody who believes in the core principle of starting with "why", and a bit of a UX buff, I find Apple products and software fascinating. My company, M3 Digital, was founded originally as a software company that develops and deploys ideas, either internally or from clients, into beautiful iPhone, iPad, and WatchOS applications.

What I Do

UX DESIGN

What we know vs. what we think our users know vs. what they really know are never the same. I believe building your foundation off of a solid UX strategy is so critical to building effective User Interfaces in 2020. This is why I always employ the full UX design process with each new project.

FULL-STACK CODING

My core strategy when developing & deploying any software is to implement Agile principles towards delivering an exceptional user experience to anybody in the world, on any device. I have built a lot of websites on many different platforms. However, I'm most skilled in HTML5, CSS3, JS , & PHP.

E-COMMERCE SETUP

Wordpress and Shopify are my stomping grounds. I can set up an e-commerce store from start to finish via Wordpress, Shopify, or HTML5. This allows my clients to serve their customers remotely in today's global economy. My shops are designed from the ground up to wow users on any device.

MOBILE APP DESIGNS

Creating mobile app designs is straight up fun. What started in Adobe XD led to me discovering Sketch, which has now been succeeded by Figma. The prototypes I build are designed to be fun & engaging with a clean, polished UI. I've included several examples of my work below for you to view.
